Project: PongDashDuel
Engine: Unreal Engine 5.x

Controls:
Player 1: W/S
Player 2: Up/Down
Restart: R

Win Condition:
First player to 7 points wins.

Original Mechanic:
1. Momentum Pickups - random power-up items spawn in the play field and are activated by the ball. The effect is assigned based on the last player who hit the ball, encouraging players to aim shots toward pickups for tactical advantage.
2. Danger Area - Tornado
